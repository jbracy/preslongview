Historical Dig

On Friday, March 21, 2008, a small group of volunteers gathered to investigate what may be below the surface of the grounds surrounding the historic Campbell house located on South Center Street in Longview. This group was led by two archeological stewards from the Texas Historical Commission, Bryan Boyd and Mark Walters. They were assisted by two students from the LeTourneau University History Department, Ryan Reynolds and Frank Batham.
